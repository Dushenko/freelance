My transactions to the uplines

Profit loss - is a purchase of a level by your referral of the appropriate line, if you haven't reached this level yet. In this case the funds will be directed to Your upline of the appropriate line, from whom you can buy this level. For example. Referral of the 3rd line wants to buy the 3rd level from You but you still haven't bought the 2nd level. In this case the money will be directed to your upline of the 3rd line.

Profit loss arises in 2 cases:

1) If you bought the 1st level, and each subsequent one you buy gradually from the funds already received from the System, but your referrals buy, ahead of time, several levels at once. This is a normal situation, foreshadowing coming rapid growth. The Profit loss of your referrals, appearing in the same way, will eventually become your SUPERPROFIT and will increase your income many times over. From the 2nd year you will receive all payments from each referral for each level.

2) If you do not buy levels on time or do not re-buy levels after a year participation. In this case, the money that you should have received will be directed to your uplines of the corresponding lines. You can buy or extend levels for any number of years and begin to make profit again at any moment. Your account remains in the System forever.

If your level is active, then even if your referral buys this level many times in a row for many years to come, all this money goes to you. If you have inactive referrals, then all the money that should have gone to them for the corresponding levels goes to you.
